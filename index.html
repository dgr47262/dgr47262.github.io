Hawaii

Overview

Out of all of the 50 states of the U.S, Hawaii can be counted as one of the most beautiful. It contains some of the most diverse ecosystems as well as some of the most amazing views. Ever since it became a state in the year 1959, it has become a major tourist attraction and vacation spot. In this website I will explain the history of the 50th state as well as some facts about this wonderful island.

History

Like mentioned before, the island of Hawaii was made a state in the year 1959. However the history of this island stretches much farther than the 60 years since it became a state. The island was first inhabited by humans around 1000 A.D and they lived in isolation for hundreds of year until discovered by British explorer James Cook in 1778. Hawaii was then made a territory by 1898 and finally made a state by 1959.
